About the Project¶

MindMira is a psychological counseling chatbot designed for Korean university students. Its mission is to promote self-reflection and emotional awareness in a safe and non-judgmental environment.

Key Features¶

  • Easy-to-use chatbot interface.

  • Emotion tracking and analysis.

  • Personalized feedback and suggestions.
